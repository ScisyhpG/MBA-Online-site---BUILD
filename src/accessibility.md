### 1. Our Commitment
King's Own Institute (KOI) is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and actively working to apply the relevant accessibility standards to our digital platforms, including this Online MBA portal.

### 2. Conformance Status
We strive to adhere to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and more user-friendly for everyone. 

### 3. Accessibility Features
To ensure our website is accessible, we have implemented the following features:
* **Semantic HTML:** Use of proper heading structures, lists, and landmarks to assist screen readers.
* **Color Contrast:** High contrast ratios between text and background colors (such as our KOI brand dark blue and white themes) to aid readability.
* **Keyboard Navigation:** Ensuring interactive elements like forms, menus, and application buttons are navigable using a keyboard.
* **Alternative Text:** Providing descriptive `alt` text for images and logos.

### 4. Feedback and Support
We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of the KOI Online MBA website. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please let us know so we can make the necessary improvements.

### 1. Introduction
AIBM trading as King's Own Institute ("KOI", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and manage your personal data when you visit our Online MBA landing page or interact with our digital services, in accordance with the *Privacy Act 1988* (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

### 2. The Information We Collect
We may collect, store, and transfer different kinds of personal information about you, including:
* **Identity & Contact Data:** First name, last name, email address, and phone number provided when downloading course guides or applying.
* **Educational Data:** Academic history and professional background submitted during the application process.
* **Technical Data:** Internet protocol (IP) address, browser type, time zone setting, and operating system collected via standard web analytics.

### 3. How We Use Your Information
We will only use your personal information for the following purposes:
* To process your inquiries or applications for the Online MBA program.
* To provide you with requested information, such as brochures or curriculum details.
* To improve our website, services, marketing, and user experiences.
* To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations as a registered Australian higher education provider.

### 4. Data Security & Storage
We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. Access to your personal data is limited to KOI employees, contractors, and agents who have a legitimate business need to know.

### 5. Your Legal Rights
Under Australian privacy law, you have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to ask for corrections if it is inaccurate. To exercise these rights, or if you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **admissions@koi.edu.au**.
